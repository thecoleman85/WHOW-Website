Frequently Asked Questions

What is WHOW?

Founded in October 2010, WHOW is a local grassroots organization of Rochester women wtih the common goal of helping women in Rochester and around the world.

How do I join WHOW?

Visit our contact us page HERE and fill out the form.

I'm not in your state. How can I be involved?

WHOW is not a non-profit. We simply support existing nonprofits. Go to the See The Impact page and donate to any of the organizations listed.

How do I donate to WHOW?

We are not a nonprofit and do not accept donations. We suggest donating directly to the organizations mentioned on this website.

I'm not a woman. How can I be involved?

Check out any of our causes. Make donations in whatever way best suits you.
