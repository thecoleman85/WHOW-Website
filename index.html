We believe there are many ways to give...
Caring, educating others, personal donations, fundraising, and giving time, talents, and treasures.
WHOW started around a dining room table. We brainstormed about what each of us felt passionately about in terms of issues in the world affecting women.
By the end of the first meeting, we agreed that we wanted to educate ourselves and others. But we wanted to be a group about ACTION.
So, each month we do ACTION ITEMS for local and international nonprofits that help women.
